ABOUT PFS

P.F School Education System(PFS), established in 1994 in Karachi, is now a chain of schools with 4 campuses in Karachi.We are affiliated with Board of Secondary Education (BSEK). Most of our students are studying in best universities and doing job in international firms and companies. We have approximately 4000+ students studying in P.F School Education System. Also, more than 3000 families are registered on our social networking pages and website. P.F Schools Education System (abbreviated as PFS) is one of the largest private English medium school systems in Karachi established in 1994. It offers preschool, primary education, secondary education and preparation for local SSC. PFS has earned its outstanding reputation through its students’ successes, its rigorous educational programs and its excellent and dedicated staff. It also promotes and encourages co-curricular activities in all of its campuses.

OUR VISION
